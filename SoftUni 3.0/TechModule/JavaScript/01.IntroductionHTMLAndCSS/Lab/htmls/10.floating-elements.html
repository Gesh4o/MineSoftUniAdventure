Left alignment

The float CSS property specifies that an element should be taken from the normal flow and placed along the left or right side of its container, where text and in-line elements will wrap it around.

Right alignment

The float CSS property specifies that an element should be taken from the normal flow and placed along the left or right side of its container, where text and in-line elements will wrap it around.

Without floating, the inline-block elements float like words in a text and block elements just do not float and stay in vertical block of full width.

No alignment

Without floating, the inline-block elements float like words in a text and block elements just do not float and stay in vertical block of full width.
